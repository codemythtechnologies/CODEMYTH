THE AI ERA

Software is being rebuilt around AI. We build with it, not around it.

AI has moved from novelty to infrastructure — teams that use it well now ship faster, test more thoroughly, and catch problems before customers do. We treat AI as part of the engineering process itself, not a bolt-on chatbot feature.

In practice, that means AI pair-programming tools scaffold the repetitive parts of a build, LLMs generate edge-case test suites a human would take days to write by hand, and automation handles the checks that used to eat a QA engineer's whole afternoon. A person still reviews and merges every line — AI sets the pace, engineers set the standard.
